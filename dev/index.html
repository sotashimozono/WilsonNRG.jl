WilsonNRG.friedel_pin — Method
friedel_pin(; Γ)

πΓ·A(0) for the U=0 symmetric resonant level. Equals the general Friedel/Fermi-liquid pinning value sin²(π⟨n_σ⟩) evaluated at half-filling (⟨n_σ⟩ = 1/2 ⇒ 1).

Grounded references (docs/refs): Weichselbaum & von Delft, PRL 99, 076402 (2007) — "πΓ A^exact{T=0} = sin²(π⟨c†0σ c0σ⟩0)"; Bulla, Costi & Pruschke, RMP 80, 395 (2008).

WilsonNRG.resonant_level_spectral — Method
resonant_level_spectral(ω; Γ)

Exact impurity spectral function A(ω) of the U=0 symmetric Anderson model (resonant level), hybridization width Γ. Lorentzian of half-width Γ.

WilsonNRG.spectral_sum_rule — Method
spectral_sum_rule(; Γ, W = 1e4Γ, n = 2_000_001)

Numerically integrate ∫ A(ω) dω (trapezoid over [-W, W]) for the U=0 resonant level. Exact value is 1. Grounded reference (docs/refs): Bulla, Costi & Pruschke, RMP 80, 395 (2008) — "∫ dω A_σ(ω,T) = 1 is, by construction, fulfilled exactly".
